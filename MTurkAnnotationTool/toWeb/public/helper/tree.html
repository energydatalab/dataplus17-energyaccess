Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What counts as part of a tree?
    • We would like you to count any part of the branches or leaves as part of the tree. Please leave out the shadow however.
  2. What if it looks like there are multiple trees together?
    • Try to draw a polygon around each individual tree. Use your best judgement when it is difficult to discern if a tree or two are seperate or disjoint.
  3. What if part of the tree is on the border of the image?
    • Draw a border around the portions of the image which are in view like the second example image below.
