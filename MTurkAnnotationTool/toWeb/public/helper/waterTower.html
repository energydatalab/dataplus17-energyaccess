Water towers

Identification

What does a water tower look like?

Water towers can be hard to spot. Often, they just appear as a circle. Some tips for identifying water towers:

  • Look for large circular structures with a smaller ring on top. Many water towers have a railing around the top that is visible in these aerial images.
  • Look for shadows. While not all pictures have good shadows, a large number do. Use these to your advantage.
