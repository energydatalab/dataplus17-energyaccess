Finding the Lamp Post

Often, the lamp post is not actually visible, but its shadow is. Here is a sample procedure we use to annotate lamp posts:

  1. Find the lamp post's shadow in an image.
  2. Looking at buildings, determine which way the shadows fall
  3. Identify where the shadow originates from
  4. Annotate a point at the base of a shadow
