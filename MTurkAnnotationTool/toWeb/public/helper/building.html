Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What if a building is on the edge of this image?
      2. Draw a polygon around the parts that you can see.
  2. What do we do with small roofed structures like shacks or outhouses?
      3. Annotate them anyway!
  3. If two buildings seem connected by a narrow pathway, should I annotate them with two separate polygons or under one polygon?
      4. Either way is fine. Please make sure that the connection between the two buildings is inside the polygon however.
