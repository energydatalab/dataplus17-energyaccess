What do industrial complexes look like?

Industrial complexes have a great variety in their appearance. We are trusting you to recognize them â€“ if you believe something is an industrial complex, then label it as one. Since appearance can vary wildly between two complexes, it is hard to give tips that apply for every or almost every industrial complex. Here are some that hopefully help you:

  • Look for large areas with many large buildings or structures inside.
  • Look for steam or smoke emissions.
  • Some industrial complexes have railroad extensions connecting to them.
